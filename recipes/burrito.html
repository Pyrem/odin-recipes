Burrito

Description

The Beef Filling is the main flavour component in this burrito recipe. It’s highly seasoned and juicy so it provides the “wetness” in the burrito as well as the flavour mixing through the other ingredients when you roll it up.

The following is a recipe for a beef burrito.

Ingredients

Burrito Seasoning

  • 1 tsp each onion powder
  • 2 tsp each dried cumin powder
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper

Beef

  • 1/2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • ½ onion
  • 500g / 1 lb beef mince (ground beef)
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 3 tbsp water

Burritos

  • 6 - 8 large soft flour tortillas or round wraps
  • 3 cups cooked rice
  • 3 cups iceberg lettuce or cabbage
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 cup black beans
  • 3 tomatoes, deseeded and diced
  • 1/2 red onion
  • Finely chopped coriander / cilantro
  • 1 1/2 cups / 150g shredded cheese of choice
